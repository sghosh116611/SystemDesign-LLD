> Strategy Design Pattern is a behavioral design pattern

Use Case :-

> We define certain functions/algorithms and provide it to the client.
> Now, we want to allow the client applications to decide which service they want to use.
> Example :- 
1) A merchant can pay via cash or cheque. 
2) We want to allow the various merchants to decide their payment method
3) We create the various payment algorithms
4) We pass this service to the merchant for it to use.
